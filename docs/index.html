Community

Hackathon - Winner

February 2019 - BCGDV - DEPAUL - London - Hacking homelessness

Utilising my idea, my 3 teammates and I worked tirelessly to produce AskPaul: A communication framework targeting youth on the verge of homelessness. By considering the needs and symptoms of unprepared homelessness, we were able to target a massive problem area. The solution we produced was intentionally branded to be un-accusatory and intended to be used by those who do not consider themselves homeless. We chose a tech stack that was cheap and easy to implement with little to no hosting overhead, ensuring databases and confronting sign up workflows were avoided.
